Gallery of Hindu Gods

Lord Shiva

The destroyer of evil and the transformer, known as the Supreme Being who creates, protects, and transforms the universe.

Lord Vishnu

The preserver and protector of the universe, known for his ten incarnations to restore cosmic order.

Goddess Durga

Represents feminine power and strength, known for destroying evil forces and protecting the righteous.

Ancient Spirituality and Beliefs

Hinduism, one of the oldest religions, revolves around the concept of Dharma, Karma, and the eternal truth. Ancient Hindu texts, like the Vedas and Upanishads, explore the fundamental questions of existence, consciousness, and the universe. Discover how spirituality connects with the cosmic forces and how gods symbolize aspects of the divine and human nature.
